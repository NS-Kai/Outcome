  4. Describe common development methodologies used by modern project managers and identify its advantages and disadvantages.
    • Scrum will be the common development methodologies for modern project. It require two team to start the project, which the first team need a scrum master, product owner and development team. The second team will have user, slakeholder and manager. First, the product owner will give ideas, and scrum master need to assign the teammate to handle each part of ideas and hand it in about one to two weeks. They need to discuss about this project everyday, state about what had encountered and the fault. After that, they will start to do another ideas until all the ideas was done, then they can start to increment the product.
      This method is flexible, which can be go back to the previous manner when encounter some fault. And also, this method also provide teamwork to complete the task, which faster the process. However, this method need to have discussion every morning, and require every teammate to say about what had done yesterday. It may be not accepted by every teammates, as everyone has their own characteristic.
